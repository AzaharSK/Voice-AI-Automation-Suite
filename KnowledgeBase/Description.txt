About Fork & Dagger
Tucked discreetly in the heart of Knightsbridge, Fork & Dagger invites you on an unforgettable culinary journey. With two Michelin stars and an ever-evolving menu, we transform the finest seasonal ingredients into masterpieces of taste and artistry. From the mysterious alchemy of flavors to the artful presentation on your plate, every detail is designed to awaken your senses.
Step into a world where elegance meets innovation, and every bite tells a story waiting to be savored. Fork & Dagger is more than dining—it's an invitation to indulge in the extraordinary.
